Teams

Or Accounts, as we call them for now.

Coming soon!

Every Outkit account is currently a team with a single member, but that’s behind the scenes - we don’t show this in the UI yet.

Simply put, we wanted to get the product out without waiting for a team membership UI. Everything about Outkit has been designed to support teams with multiple members, and we will soon have a very nice UI to invite new members and manage roles, but in the meantime we wanted to get Outkit out there.

So, pardon the confusion and please bear with us - teams are coming soon!
